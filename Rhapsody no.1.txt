<p align="center"><em>Allegretto con Passione e Tenerezza</em></p>
<p align="center">***</p>
<p>The flickering candlelight cast faint shadows across the vast study of the castle. The tiny flames seemed to tremble against the chill radiating from the ancient stone walls. Anna Shcherbakova sat behind her heavy oak desk, her brow furrowing slightly as she turned the pages of her documents. Tonight, she wanted no disturbances. She required privacy to untangle the thoughts consuming her mind.</p>

<p>Distant, soft footsteps suddenly echoed down the corridor. Anna recognized them instantly. Despite the intentional lightness, the stride bore a distinctive weight—and a familiar hunger.</p>

<p>She did not look up.</p>

<p>The heavy wooden door was pushed open without a knock. A draft of cold air swept into the room, carrying the faint, metallic tang of dried blood and Sasha’s signature heavy musk.</p>

<p>"Good evening, Mistress."</p>

<p>The greeting broke the innate silence of the darkened room, illuminated only by a few lingering candles. Without lifting her eyes from her paperwork, Anna already knew the owner of that unmistakable voice. The vampire was misbehaving again tonight.</p>

<p>"I did not summon you at this hour, Sasha," the young lady spoke, her tone as frigid as the one she habitually reserved for her servants. "If you find yourself with too much idle time, you might consider assisting Ilya in the kitchen; I believe he could use another pair of hands."</p>

<p>Contrary to Anna's expectations, no grating, raspy reply followed. Instead, the only sound answering the young lady was the click of the door as it shut. <em>Something is amiss,</em> the young noblewoman thought briefly, knowing the vampire's temperament. Trusova would never enter this room without some playful banter or pretentious, pseudo-philosophical remarks designed to vex her. But if she was silent now, something grave must have transpired.</p>

<p>As Anna lowered her documents, the vampire's face came into view, and she instantly caught the wrongness in those gaunt, haggard features. The whites of her eyes were bloodshot, her pupils terrifyingly constricted, and her breathing was shallow. Her body seemed to weigh a few stones more as she failed to stand steadily, swaying against the doorframe. Had this been an ordinary servant, Anna wouldn't have hesitated to throw her into the dungeon, for these were the textbook signs of withdrawal. But the damnation of it was that Sasha was a vampire; the thing she craved was not a drug, but something far more feral.</p>

<p>"Blood..." Sasha rasped heavily, wheezing. "I need it..."</p>

<p>"Do you want me to take a pair of pliers and rip those wretched fangs right out of your precious jaw, Alexandra Trusova? I am busy, and I have no time for your antics. Begone."</p>

<p>"Blood..." As if deaf to the fine words the brunette had just uttered, Sasha stumbled toward the wooden desk, her breathing growing heavier. "I need it... give it to me... please."</p>

<p>Inwardly, Anna truly wanted to curse—a profound, venomous profanity darker than any oath in the Russian tongue. By the merciful Virgin Mary, she wanted nothing more than to summon her guards, hand them pliers, and have them shatter every single glistening, foolish tooth in the vampire's mouth. Then Sasha would have plenty of blood in her own mouth to drink.</p>

<p>But the young lady could not do it. Sasha's prowess was undeniable, and she needed that strength for her impending plans, as well as to safeguard the castle. Moreover, to be fair, it had been days since this foolish fledgling had fed according to her nutritional needs, and it was Anna herself—no one else—who had indulged the servant's spoiled behavior.</p>

<p>"Come here," the girl murmured, flicking her long wood-brown hair to one side—a terse, commanding order. Yet, it was more than enough for the crimson-haired servant trembling in the center of the room.</p>

<p>The new moon cycle—or whatever foolish name the bespectacled scholars rotting away in university libraries gave it—was a physiological phase common among vampires, usually striking mid-month. During this time, they had to obtain fresh, hot human blood at all costs before the withdrawal forced them into a self-destructive frenzy. Not the foul blood of beasts, and certainly not the rancid fluid of corpses. The blood required to keep them from tearing themselves apart had to be the searing liquid coursing through the arteries of a higher living organism—specifically, a human.</p>

<p>Domesticated mages usually underwent rigorous, lengthy training to suppress this urge, and in most cases, they no longer relapsed during the new moon. But nothing was guaranteed, and all that discipline could be thrown to the dogs if a domesticated vampire accidentally tasted the blood of an adult human. And last time, in the haze of a wild revelry, Sasha's fangs had accidentally torn the flesh of Anna's chest, letting a single drop of hot blood touch that long tongue. Ironically, that too had been a day in the middle of the month...</p>

<p>Sasha's fangs grazed the carotid artery throbbing frantically against her mistress's neck, nuzzling the soft skin she so deeply craved. The maid gently lowered her long tongue, sweeping it across the skin with reverent care, like a nurse swabbing alcohol before an injection—except this "nurse" looked more like an addict, her hot breath involuntarily washing over Anna's collarbone. The vampire played discreetly with the porcelain skin for a moment longer before her fangs located their target, pressing just enough to pierce and...</p>

<p><em>Sink.</em></p>

<p>It would be a lie to say Anna wasn't afraid when she saw those massive fangs looming over her, especially at such a vulnerable spot. But once they settled deep within her, and the warmth of the maid's mouth softened the agony of the bite, the lady's rigid frame finally relaxed. A soothing ease washed over her; her muscles uncoiled, and a heavy, dreamlike drowsiness took hold. Deep within her soul, a forbidden thought whispered: <em>Don't stop.</em> It clung to her, growing louder and heavier as that large hand didn't remain still but began a journey of its own, tracing the line of her spine with a featherlight caress, before slithering like a serpent lower past her waist and...</p>

<p>Anna’s consciousness abruptly snapped back, and she realized something was deeply wrong. She tried to push Sasha away, tried to use her commanding voice to give Trusova a proper reprimand. But the push dissolved into a desperate clutch at Sasha’s lapels, pulling her closer, while her scolding melted into a soft, broken moan.</p>

<p>"You have endured quite enough," that familiar, raspy voice murmured, though to Anna, it sounded as if it echoed from a vast distance. "Let me care for you from now on, my Mistress."</p>

<p>Sasha’s words hung in the air like a velvet fog, enveloping Anna’s senses while her fangs remained embedded deep in the neck of the Shcherbakova heiress, drawing slow, rhythmic drafts of blood. Each swallow sent a wave of numbness—a searing warmth spilling through her veins, radiating outward from the wound like a slow burn beneath the skin, dissolving the lady's resistance into soft, pliant submission. The metallic tang of blood mingled with Sasha's breath, a sharp copper scent filling the room, utterly dominated by the heavy, earthy musk radiating from the maid's body, scalding and intoxicatingly close.</p>

<p>Sasha's large hand, calloused from battles yet surprisingly tender, drifted lower. Her fingertips traced the curve of Anna's spine with a featherlight touch, causing her to arch her back involuntarily. The linen of her uniform shirt murmured softly against her skin as Sasha tugged it free from her waistband, exposing Anna’s flushed flesh to the cool air of the room. Goosebumps erupted along her lower back, a stark contrast to the burning heat radiating from Sasha's palm as it cupped her hip, pulling Anna flush against the maid's frame. Anna could feel the burning heat of Sasha's core through the thin fabric of the servant's trousers—a pressing, urgent throb that synchronized with her own racing pulse, echoing in her ears like distant church bells.</p>

<p>Anna's breath hitched, turning into an involuntary gasp right against Sasha's ear. The hands that had intended to push her away were now tangled in the coarse fabric of the maid's shirt, her knuckles white from the strain. The world narrowed down to the tongue softly licking the punctures between drafts of blood, soothing the pain with a wet, tender gentleness that sent shivers racing down her arms. Sasha's free hand rose to cradle the back of Anna's neck, her fingers threading through the brown hair, silky strands brushing against the maid's feverish skin. Sasha tilted Anna’s head slightly, driving her fangs a fraction deeper—just enough to evoke a new, profound sensation, a secret yearning her mistress had always harbored but could never voice.</p>

<p>"Sasha..." Anna whispered, her voice entirely raspy, her maid's name slipping out effortlessly amidst the moan. A salty tang lingered on her lips where she had bitten them, and she could smell the faint trace of gunpowder on Sasha's clothes—a remnant of the day's hunt—now blending with the primal, intimate aroma of their shared warmth. Sasha's body pressed closer against Anna's, a low rumble vibrating from the maid's chest, echoing through her mistress like thunder, as though their hearts were beating as one. Slowly and deliberately, Sasha withdrew her fangs with a wet, sharp pull, followed by a trickle of warm blood sliding down Anna's neck. Sasha immediately licked it clean, her lips pressing against the wound in a deep, burning kiss that left Anna’s skin more sensitive than ever before.</p>

<p>The maid's hands grew bolder; one hand slid beneath the shirt to cradle her full breast, her thumb swirling over the hardened peak through the delicate barrier of her undergarments...</p>
