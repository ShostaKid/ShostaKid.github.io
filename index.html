About the Author

Welcome. I write under the name ShostaKid — a name borrowed from a composer whose music once broke something open in me and never quite let it close again.

My works live in the space between classical music and fanfiction: dark, passionate, and sometimes deeply uncomfortable. I believe that literature, like music, should move you — even when the movement is painful.

I write primarily for the Reverse: 1999 fandom and occasionally for figure skating RPF. My fics tend toward dark themes, non-linear structure, and an obsession with what it means to love someone who can destroy you.

All works are also posted on Archive of Our Own. If something here moves you, please leave a comment there — it means more than you know.
